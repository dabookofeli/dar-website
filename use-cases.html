Use cases

Digital Action Receipts are useful wherever a system later needs a neutral reference for: what happened, when, and by whom/what — without turning logs into a courtroom.

DARs are strongest when the question is occurrence (proof a specific action happened at a moment), not correctness (whether it was the right decision).
